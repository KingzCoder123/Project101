About Us

The Cinnamon Co. is a Cape Town-based home bakery dedicated to creating handcrafted cinnamon rolls made with love. We focus on quality ingredients, fresh baking, and premium flavors that turn every order into a special treat. We believe every cinnamon roll should be worth slowing down for. Each roll is freshly baked, made to order, and crafted with quality ingredients to bring you soft, fluffy layers and unforgettable flavor. Every bite is made with love.
